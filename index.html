Welcome to Furry Friends Adoption Centre. Here you will find a wide range of animals to adopt.

The process of adopting with us is easy! Come visit our Shelter to meet our furry friends. Once you find your perfect fit,you will be days away from taking your new friend home.

Our brand new additions to our shelter are listed below and can't wait to meet you:

Sparky

Sparky is a 2-year-old yellow lab. He loves long walks and playing fetch with his tennis ball. His favorite toy is a squeaky rubber ball. He is great with kids, loves to cuddle, and needs a home!

Kitty

Kitty is a 1-year-old cat with an attitude. She loves people but also isn't afraid to be alone. She loves watching cars go by and her kitty food. She loves to be pet behind her ears. Kitty would be a great addition to a busy family.

Leo

Leo is a a 2-year-old lion with a big heart. He loves to run and loves to wrestle. He is very patient so no need to worry about leaving Leo alone with your kids. Leo loves to watch tv making him a great addition to any household.

Owen

Owen is a 4-year-old spotted owl. He is wise beyond his years and very intelligent. He likes high flights in the sky and enjoys the moonlight. Own would be the perfect fit for anyone needing a companion.
